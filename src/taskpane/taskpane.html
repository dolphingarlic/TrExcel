How to play

  • Clear rows using the colourful pieces, just like how Microsoft Excel clears away all your troubles.
  • Don't let the rows reach the top of the game board, just like how you don't let anyone diss Microsoft Excel.
  • Fill rows completely to clear them, just like how Microsoft Excel fills you with joy.
  • Clear multiple rows at once to get more points, just like how Microsoft Excel gives you more stuff.
  • Microsoft Excel has as many cool things as a rainbow has colours (at least 5), so clear rows with many colours to get even more points!

Controls

  • ←: move the piece left.
  • →: move the piece right.
  • ↓: move the piece down.
  • space: drop the piece like it's hot.
  • ↑ or X: rotate the piece clockwise.
  • ctrl or Z: rotate the piece counterclockwise.
  • S: rotate the piece 180 degrees.
  • D: change the piece's colour, just like how Microsoft Excel changes your life.
  • shift or C: pocket the piece.
  • P: pause/resume the game.
